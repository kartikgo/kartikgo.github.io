KARTIK GOYAL ☰

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech starting Fall 2023.

NOTE: I am looking for PhD. students. If you are interested in working with me please apply to a PhD. Program offered by the School of Interactive Computing.

I am interested in Language Modeling, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning problems pertaining to modeling data with structured random variables for development of efficient, interpretable, and controllable probabilistic models. I am also excited about the intersection of these problems with various applications in Digital Humanities research.

Contact: kartikgo@gatech.edu
