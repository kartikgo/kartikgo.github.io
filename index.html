I am a Research Assistant Professor at Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago.

I recently finished my Ph.D. at Language Technologies Institute, School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, for which I was jointly advised by Chris Dyer and Taylor Berg-Kirkpatrick. I am interested in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning problems pertaining to modeling data with structured random variables for development of efficient, interpretable, and controllable probabilistic models.

Contact: kartikgo@ttic.edu
